How to Play

  • Your goal: Score maximum points by guessing the mystery word within 60 seconds while using the fewest number of hints.
  • Your reward: Ranking on the leaderboard (if you're logged in) and bragging rights as the nerdiest of the Werd Nerdz!
  • You may guess as many times as you want to wihtout a penalty.
  • You'll start with 100 points and a few freebie hints about the mystery word. Points will be deducted as you use additional hints:
    • 1st hint: 50 points deducted
    • 2nd hint: 25 more points deducted
    • 3rd hint: 15 more points deducted

Did we mention that the clock will be ticking?

You'll have 60 seconds to guess the mystery word.
If the time runs out: sorry, no points for you! Are you ready?
